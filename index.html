Help & FAQ

The QCM Answer Tracker is a tool to record your answers and track your performance across various lessons and question series. Use the session section to select a lesson and enter the series name before answering questions. Your results and performance are tracked in real time.

To save a session, choose a lesson from the dropdown, enter the series name, and then answer your questions. Click "Save Session" to store your answers along with time metrics and scores. Each session captures details like total attempts, correct/incorrect/partial counts, and timing information.

You can export all saved session data as a JSON file by clicking "Export Data." To import data, click "Import Data" and select your JSON file. Importing data will override your current local storage data.

The Dashboard displays all lessons you’ve studied along with the series names. Click the "View Stats" button next to any series to see aggregated statistics, including total questions answered, correct/incorrect/partial counts, average score (scaled from 0 to 20), and average time per question.

This app is a Progressive Web App (PWA) that can be installed on your device for an app-like experience. On Android, Chrome may prompt you to add the app to your home screen. On iOS, use the "Share" menu to "Add to Home Screen." If you have issues, verify that your manifest and service worker are correctly linked and that you are accessing the site over HTTPS.
